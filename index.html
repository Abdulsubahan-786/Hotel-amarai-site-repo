A Sanctuary in Kondigre

Welcome to Hotel Aamarai Resorts & Food Court. Located near Ideal College in Kondigre, our resort offers a unique getaway experience featuring iconic red-brick architecture and sprawling green lawns.


Whether you're visiting for our famous food court delicacies, planning a family stay in our luxury rooms, or hosting a special event in our gardens, we ensure a peaceful and premium atmosphere for every guest.
